Hey there! I am Karen and I love creating websites using clean code and design patterns, and the latest web technologies. I like coding my own designs from scratch with modern HTML, super organised CSS and modular, object-oriented Javascript, but also make use of content management systems such as Wordpress to bring your ideas to life.

Furthermore, I am proficient in PHP, Python, MySQL and Node.js and am able to leverage NPM and build tools such as Gulp and Webpack. As a developer I write code that conforms with industry standards and is in line with best practices.

I pay close attention to the detail and I believe that each website must be original with great characteristics and of high quality in order to make your life easier and serve your needs. My belief is that I will never build a website that is not good enough to use myself.

I am somewhat of a perfectionist and therefore I am strongly committed to continuous learning and improvement.

Features

We’ll Watch the Weather

Download our app and we’ll send you a notice if it’s about to rain in the next 20 minutes around your current location. A good rain can be refreshing, but sometimes you just want to stay dry.

Global Guides

We’ve scoured the entire planet for the best retreats and beautiful vistas. If there’s a corner of the world you want to escape to we know the most scenic and inspiring locations.

Wi-Fi Waypoints

We only send you on trips to places we can personally vouch for as being amazing. Which means we’ve mapped out where local wi-fi spots are and marked them in our app’s map view.

Survival Kit

Everytime you book an escape with us we send you a survival kit with the finest materials. The kit will allow you to setup a tent, start a fire, scratch your own back and lower your taxes.
